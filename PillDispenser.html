Pill Dispenser

Pill Pal is is a pill dispensing robot pal made by a team of 8 product development engineers that makes taking medication with kids all the more safe and consistent. Pill pal will make sure kids don't get to their pills when they don't need to while also promoting healthy habits. As mentioned prior, competitors lack a fun and interactive user experience while we bring both the fun and the interaction with our LCD screen and robot pal design.
